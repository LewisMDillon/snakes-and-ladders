How To Play


Before starting the game, select your desired number of players in the Game Setup area. Choosing 1 player will create a game against a computer opponent and selecting 2 players will create a game where you and a friend can take turns and play together.

Pick the colour(s) you would like to play with and then start the game!

During the game, click the roll button on your turn to roll the dice, your piece will then advance automatically.

If you're playing against the computer, it will take its turns automatically.

Press the reset game button to start the game over again.

Watch out for snakes!



Rules

  1. The first player to reach the FINISH square wins.
  2. If your piece lands at the bottom of a ladder, you climb up!
  3. If your piece lands at the top of a snake, you slide down!
  4. When you are within 1 dice roll of the finish square, if you roll more than you need, you do not move and instead skip your turn.
